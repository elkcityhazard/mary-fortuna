Sculpture and Other Art made by Mary Fortuna

Sculpture, dolls, puppets, paintings, strange objects and other weirdness.

Artist Statement

I make puppets and dolls and animals and fantastic creatures of all kinds. I work in any medium that catches my interest, two or three dimensional, to make whatever I can conjure up. Iâ€™m more interested in the objects themselves than in any single, specific narrative. My visual vocabulary draws from world mythology, the spirit world as conceived by people everywhere, and the infinite variety of life forms that make up the natural and supernatural worlds. In recent years I have concentrated mostly on fabric and textiles. Many works take the form of flags or banners, embellished with embroidery, beads, buttons, found objects, and other materials. Other works include dolls, puppets, masks, mobiles, and three-dimensional human and animal figures. I go back to painting and collage on paper, canvas, or wood panels when it suits me.

Mary Fortuna

September 2021

About The Artist

Mary Fortuna recently moved to Traverse City from the Detroit area. Currently, she works primarily in soft sculpture and textiles, and has exhibited her work all over Michigan, as well as nationally and internationally.
